KNOW THE ROLES

DUELISTS

Duelists in Valorant are aggressive attackers who focus on securing kills and leading the charge in fights. Their primary role is to create space for their team by eliminating enemies and pushing forward. They are equipped with abilities that allow them to take risks, such as healing, dashing, or using flashes to disorient opponents. Duelists are essential for initiating attacks and putting pressure on the enemy. Key Duelists in Valorant include Phoenix, Jett, and Reyna.



Initiators

Initiators in Valorant are crucial for disrupting the enemy team and creating openings for their squad. They use abilities that can disorient, scout, or force enemies to reposition, making it easier for teammates to push or defend. Initiators excel at gathering information and initiating fights, often leading the charge with utilities like flashes, drones, or stuns. Their role is to break through enemy defenses and create opportunities for successful engagements. Examples of Initiators include Breach, Sova, and Skye.



Sentinels

Sentinels in Valorant are defensive experts focused on protecting their team and securing key areas. They excel at holding angles, setting up traps, and providing support through healing or shielding teammates. Sentinels are crucial for locking down sites, preventing enemy pushes, and ensuring the teamâ€™s position remains safe. They are also key in clutch situations, offering utility that can turn the tide of a round. Notable Sentinels in Valorant include Sage, Killjoy, and Chamber.



Controllers

Controllers in Valorant are tactical experts who manipulate the battlefield to create opportunities for their team. Their abilities focus on blocking vision, obscuring areas with smokes, and disrupting enemy movements. Controllers excel at creating space for attackers and preventing defenders from holding key positions effectively. They are essential for controlling sightlines and ensuring that the team can safely push or defend. Popular Controllers in Valorant include Omen, Brimstone, and Astra.
